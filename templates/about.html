About us

Crowd counting is a computer vision technique that involves estimating the number of people in an image or video frame. It is a challenging task due to factors such as varying sizes and shapes of individuals, occlusion, and crowd density. However, using deep learning techniques, such as convolutional neural networks, it is possible to accurately estimate the number of people in a given area. Crowd counting has many practical applications, such as crowd management, urban planning, security, and marketing. By accurately determining the number of people in a scene, businesses and organizations can make informed decisions about resource allocation, improve safety and security, and optimize crowd control.
